Injury & Rehabilitation Analysis

  • Tools: Pandas, Jupyter Notebook, Matplotlib
  • Project date: January 2026

This analysis explores how injury types, rehabilitation programs, and biomechanical traits interact to shape an athlete’s recovery journey. The visualizations highlight patterns in injury frequency, the impact of severity on rehab time, and the physical attributes, like jump height and knee angle, that tend to support faster lower‑limb recovery. By comparing rehab efficiency with biomechanical metrics, the project reveals how strengths, vulnerabilities, and training opportunities emerge across a roster.

National Women's Soccer League Assessment

  • Tools: Kaggle, Pandas, Jupyter Notebook
  • Project date: October 2025

This project examines team-level performance in the National Women’s Soccer League (NWSL) across the 2018 and 2021 seasons using publicly available Kaggle datasets. The analysis focuses on core attacking and efficiency metrics, including shots on goal per match, goal conversion rate, assists per goal, corner kick frequency, and year-over-year performance changes. Interactive visualizations surface patterns in offensive style, finishing efficiency, and tactical development over time. The resulting insights are designed to be immediately actionable for coaches, analysts, and performance strategists seeking data-informed decision-making.
